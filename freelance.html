FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

How do Next.js and SEO services benefit my business?

Next.js provides server-side rendering which significantly improves your website's SEO by making content fully visible to search engines. Combined with my dedicated SEO services, you'll benefit from faster page loads, improved Core Web Vitals scores, and higher search rankings. This translates to better visibility, more organic traffic, and ultimately increased conversions for your business.

How long does it typically take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payments to distribute costs throughout the development process.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, content changes, and technical support.

Will I be able to update the website content myself?

Absolutely! I build websites with user-friendly content management systems or custom admin panels that allow you to easily update content. I also provide documentation and training so you feel confident managing your site.

Do you help with domain registration and hosting?

Yes, I can assist with domain registration, hosting setup, and deployment. I'll recommend reliable hosting providers based on your project's specific needs and can handle the technical aspects of setting everything up.
